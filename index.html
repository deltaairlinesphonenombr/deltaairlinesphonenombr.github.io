What is the delta Airlines check-in policy?

The travelers are required to bring their identity card with their boarding pass during the check-in process that is up to 24 hours. You are allowed to check-in before 30 minutes of your flight departure time.

You can check-in through:

  • Online
  • Self-check-in kiosks
  • Ticket counter
  • Airport curbside counter at the drop-off

Online Check can be done by following these steps:

  • Go to your check-in page and visit my trips app or browser
  • Enter into your Delta account
  • Give your confirmation number
  • Print your boarding pass

You can also check-in through the Delta app

  • Download the Delta app
  • Check-in or login your account
  • Register for sky miles account
  • Add your number for confirmation
  • You will receive your boarding pass
  • Print your boarding pass

You can check-in through the Airport kiosk

  • Check-in at the airport kiosk.
  • After your arrival, enter your SkyMiles number.
  • Enter your confirmation number or eTicket number on the desk
  • You can print your boarding pass.

You can check-in through the Airport desk

  • Reach the airport
  • Go to the delta desk counter
  • There is an area clearly marked for Delta
  • They will print your boarding pass
  • You can also ask for your preferred seat
  • They will assign as per the availability of the seat.

You can also check-in at the curbside

  • You can check-in outside the airport
  • As soon as you get out of the car or are dropped off, you will locate a curb.
  • You can check your luggage
  • Get it checked in
  • You will receive a boarding pass from there itself.

What is the time for check-in at the airport?

  • You can travel within the US.

  • Check-in before 30 minutes before departure

What is the baggage policy at Delta Airlines?

  • You can carry on must not exceed 22" x 14" x 9", which is inclusive of wheels and handles.
